FEATURE 02

Crash Detection
& Emergency
Response

Sensor fusion technology detects sudden impacts and automatically initiates a 30-second countdown, alerting emergency contacts with live GPS coordinates if no user cancellation occurs.

  • Accelerometer + Gyroscope — Multi-axis impact detection
  • GPS Coordinates — Exact location sent to contacts
  • 30-Second Cancel Window — Avoids false positives
  • Automated SMS + SOS — Flashlight & audio distress signal
