ADVANCED SAFETY FEATURES

Comprehensive driver monitoring powered by cutting-edge AI technology

01

Drowsiness Detection System

A critical safety feature utilizing advanced facial analysis to monitor driver alertness in real-time and prevent accidents caused by fatigue.

TECHNICAL IMPLEMENTATION
  • MediaPipe FaceMesh integration for facial landmark detection
  • Eye Aspect Ratio (EAR) calculation for precise drowsiness measurement
  • Real-time processing with 30+ FPS performance
  • Adaptive thresholds for individual driver patterns
---
02

Crash Detection & Emergency Response

Utilizes integrated sensors to detect a significant impact and automatically initiate emergency protocols, including contacting first responders with GPS location.

SENSOR FUSION TECHNOLOGY
  • Accelerometer and gyroscope data fusion
  • GPS coordinate tracking for emergency services
  • 30-second countdown system with user cancellation
  • Automated SMS alerts with location details
---
03

Smart Trip Planning

An intelligent route system that suggests optimal paths, monitors driving performance, and integrates with fleet management tools for efficient logistics.

NAVIGATION FEATURES
  • OSMdroid integration for offline mapping
  • Real-time route optimization
  • Performance tracking and analytics
  • Trip assignment for fleet management
---
04

General Safety Alert System

A customizable alert system for various safety issues, including speeding, harsh braking, and boundary violations, ensuring proactive driver coaching.

ALERT SYSTEM
  • Progressive alert escalation
  • Visual, audio, and haptic notifications
  • Emergency contact integration
  • SOS signaling with flashlight and sound
